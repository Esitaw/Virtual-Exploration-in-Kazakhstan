Kazakh Culture

Kazakh culture is deeply rooted in nomadic traditions, hospitality, and a love for music, storytelling, and horse riding. Family and respect for elders play a vital role in society.

Hospitality

Kazakh people take pride in their hospitality, often welcoming guests with tea and traditional food.

Eagle Hunting

A centuries-old tradition where trained golden eagles hunt for food.

Yurts & Nomadic Life

Traditional portable homes (yurts) are still used by some Kazakh nomads.

Famous Kazakh Delicacies

Kazakh cuisine is hearty and meat-based, featuring dishes passed down through generations.

Beshbarmak

The national dish, meaning "five fingers," consists of boiled meat and pasta.

Shashlik

Skewered, grilled meat similar to kebabs, often served with onions and vinegar.

Kurt

Dried salty cheese balls, a favorite snack for travelers.
